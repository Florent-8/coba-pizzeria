Our Origin

Born in Queens.
Inspired by Mexico.
100% addictive.

Coba Pizzeria is the intersection of two worlds. We've taken the structural perfection of a New York slice and infused it with the vibrant, spicy, and soulful flavors of a Mexico City street market.

From our house-made chorizo to our slow-simmered birria toppings, every pizza is a celebration of the multicultural energy that makes Long Island City home. We don't just make pizza; we create a new category of comfort food.

Coba Pizzeria started with one simple goal: build pizza that feels familiar but tastes unexpected. We blended New York structure with bold Mexican flavors, keeping quality and consistency at the center.

Every dough ball is hand-stretched, every sauce is made with intention, and every topping is chosen to bring contrast and balance. From Al Pastor to birria-inspired combinations, each pie is designed to be memorable.

We are proud to serve Long Island City and all of Queens, NYC with food that feels local, warm, and full of personality.
